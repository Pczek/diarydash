what?

This is DiaryDash.com, it's a minimalistic site to write a daily diary entry. Everyday you will be presented with a blank page with just the current date as a heading. Write whatever strives your mind, nobody will know and read. There is no need for fancy wording or nitpicking grammer and spelling. The main purpose is to whipe your minds windshield, just clear your mind.

how?

Everyday you are presented with a blank page to fill. The entry is saved locally as you type. You can access all your entries in the markdown by clicking your overall entry count at the bottom of the page.

why?

As most of us, I started using a paper diary, but it was quiet annoying to write in it every morning. Writing by hand seemed way to slow, exhausting and wasteful with the resource. Next up I used tools like DayOne and shell scripts to write a daily entry, but it also turned out pretty unhandy. I finally realised I should do it in the browser, due to the fact I'am using it almost daily. So i created this page for simple input and asigned it as my first page to open a new browser session and voila.
Also my inspiration to write a journal came from:
Tim Ferris - What My Morning Journal Looks Like
Julia Cameron - Morning Pages
